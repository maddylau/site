3 Days in Santorini


Hiking all day every day. Go to the two main towns: Oia and Fira. You won't regret it. It's pretty touristy but so beautiful that you won't mind it as much. The picture above was taken in Oia. Convinced yet?

Definitely try their dessert wine: Vinsanto. It's absolutely delicious. There are plenty of wineries to visit while you're here so go try the wine there!
